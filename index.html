About the Board

Built by UTG students, for UTG students — because missing an opportunity shouldn't be down to not knowing it existed.

About the University

The University of The Gambia (UTG) is the country's first and largest public university, established in 1999. Its motto, "Knowledge for Service," guides its mission to provide accessible, high-quality higher education to Gambians and the wider West African region.

UTG operates across several campuses — including Faraba Banta, Brikama, and Kanifing — and is organized into faculties such as Humanities & Social Sciences, Science & Agriculture, Law, Business & Management Sciences, Education, and Medicine & Allied Health Sciences.

Why we built this

Every semester, UTG students miss out on life-changing internships, scholarships, and competitions simply because there was no single place to find them. Opportunities were buried in WhatsApp groups, notice boards, or word of mouth.

The UTG Opportunity Board is a centralized hub designed with HCI principles at its core — making discovery fast, filtering intuitive, and deadlines impossible to ignore.

This project was developed as part of the HCI coursework at the University of The Gambia, applying usability, accessibility, and user-centered design principles throughout.

HCI principles applied

How we put users at the center of every decision

🎯

Usability

Clean navigation, consistent layouts, and instant feedback on every action. Users always know where they are and what just happened.

♿

Accessibility

ARIA labels, keyboard navigation, sufficient color contrast, and responsive design ensure the site works for everyone.

🎨

Visual Hierarchy

Intentional use of typography scale, whitespace, and color guides the eye to deadlines, actions, and key information.

🔄

Consistency

Uniform design tokens — colors, border radii, spacing — applied across every page so users build familiarity instantly.

🛡️

Error Prevention

Real-time form validation with specific, actionable error messages. Inputs guide users before they can submit incorrectly.

📱

Responsiveness

Fully responsive layouts from 320px mobile widths to large desktops, tested at every breakpoint.
